About AlmaCircle

Our Story

AlmaCircle was born out of a shared vision among five ISB (Indian School of Business) alumni who recognized the lack of an exclusive networking platform for students and alumni from premier institutions. The founders experienced firsthand the challenges of maintaining meaningful professional connections after graduation and saw an opportunity to create a dedicated space where like-minded individuals could connect, collaborate, and grow together.

What started as a simple idea during their time at ISB has now transformed into India's premier exclusive network for affluent students and alumni, offering unique opportunities for personal and professional development.
